We collect CT Images and classify the Neurodegeneracy into three major classes

Three major classes are:


NORMAL BRAIN


"Normal" classification indicates that the brain structure appears typical and healthy.It is associated with individuals without significant neurological disorders or abnormalities.

ATROPHIC BRAIN


Atrophy refers to the shrinking of brain tissue leading to a reduction in size.It can be a result of aging or neurodegenerative diseases. It may be associated with cognitive decline and functional impairment..

NEURODEGENERATIVE BRAIN

Neurodegenerative diseases involve the progressive degeneration of the structure and function of the nervous system.It includes conditions like Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, and Huntington's disease.
